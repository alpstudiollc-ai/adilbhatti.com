The Investigation

From biblical skies to Pentagon hearings — one continuous thread.

The UFO conversation did not start in 1947. It did not start in 2017. It has been running through the human record for as long as the human record has existed — and someone finally had to put the whole thing in one book.

Ancient Aliens to Modern UFOs: The Disclosure Revolution walks the reader chronologically through a story that most readers have only ever encountered in fragments. The book opens with biblical UFO encounters and the 1561 Nuremberg sky event — historical reports of luminous objects above European skies centuries before anyone could call them "unidentified aerial phenomena." It moves through ancient astronaut theory, then into the modern era: Roswell, Rendlesham Forest, the Phoenix Lights, and the USS Nimitz Tic Tac incident. From there, the arc climbs toward the Congressional UAP disclosure hearings and the whistleblowers who turned the subject from fringe speculation into on-the-record testimony.

This is not a book of theories. It is a book of documented incidents, declassified files, and testimony under oath.

The treatment is investigative, not speculative. Declassified Pentagon documents. Congressional UAP hearing transcripts. First-hand whistleblower accounts. Historical sighting records. The book respects the reader enough to lay the evidence out and let them draw their own conclusions about where the ancient and modern threads meet.

For readers who've been following the disclosure movement and want the full backstory. For newcomers who keep hearing "UAP" on the news and want to understand what the Pentagon is actually saying. For history buffs who always suspected there was more to those ancient sky accounts. For anyone who wants a comprehensive, evidence-based companion to one of the defining conversations of our time.

What is Ancient Aliens to Modern UFOs: The Disclosure Revolution about?

It is non-fiction. The book is an investigative work drawing on declassified government documents, historical sightings, Congressional UAP testimony, and first-hand whistleblower accounts.
