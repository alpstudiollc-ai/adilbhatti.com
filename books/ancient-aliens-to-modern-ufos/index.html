The Stakes

This is bigger than the sky.

The biggest story in human history is unfolding right now, and its implications reach far beyond anything hovering above us.

If the propulsion technology behind credible UFO and UAP sightings is genuine, then aerospace itself operates on principles fundamentally different from anything the modern global economy is built on. That technology, by every credible description, appears to draw on energy sources that are effectively free and boundlessly available, outside the framework of fossil fuels and petroleum-driven capitalism that currently defines national economies, geopolitical power, and trillions of dollars of industrial infrastructure. Genuine disclosure therefore carries consequences that extend directly into energy policy, market stability, and the incentives that have historically governed what research is funded, what is published, and what is quietly shelved.

The implications do not end at the economic. Confirmation of non-human intelligence visiting Earth, across millennia, would force a reconsideration of foundational assumptions in nearly every major religious tradition, and of the dominant secular narrative about humanity's origins, uniqueness, and place in the universe. It is the kind of question that societies rarely encounter more than once in their history.

This book takes that full picture seriously, and then walks the evidence, from the earliest recorded encounters to the testimony being entered into the Congressional record today.

Part One · Ancient Evidence

Long before flight existed, they were already in the sky.

Ancient civilizations across continents recorded encounters with beings descending from the heavens, built monuments aligned with the stars, and preserved accounts of advanced vehicles in sacred texts. The book walks these records carefully, and asks whether they describe myth or eyewitness.

The evidence is older than any modern theory of extraterrestrial life.

  • Sumerian Anunnaki TabletsThe ancient Sumerians wrote of the Anunnaki, beings described as descending from the heavens and shaping the course of human civilization.
  • Egyptian Star-Aligned MonumentsEgyptian architecture built with precise astronomical alignment, encoded into pyramids and temples that have stood for thousands of years.
  • Amarna-Period SculpturesDuring Akhenaten's reign approximately 3,300 years ago, royal portraiture shifted almost overnight to depict elongated skulls and proportions unseen before or since.
  • Ezekiel's Wheels Within WheelsA biblical account describing wheels within wheels blazing across the sky, read by some as metaphor and by others as direct observation.
  • Hindu ScriptureSanskrit texts that describe celestial beings and advanced vehicles in remarkable technical detail.
  • The QuranReferences to celestial beings and advanced means of travel that predate any known human aeronautical capability.
  • The Nazca LinesVast geoglyphs carved into the Peruvian desert, designed to be viewed from above at a time when flight was not available.
Part Two · Historic Documented Cases

Centuries of sightings, recorded in ink and on film.

Before radar and jet fighters, civilizations still wrote down what they saw. Chronicles, broadsheets, period photography, and military records document sightings that defy easy explanation.

Documented cases, long before the modern era.

  • 1561 Nuremberg Celestial BattleContemporary German broadsheets reported a mass aerial event witnessed by the entire city, depicted in surviving illustrations.
  • 1896-1897 Great Airship MysteryWaves of mysterious airship sightings across the United States at a time when no such aircraft existed anywhere in the world.
  • 1942 Battle of Los AngelesOne of the most significant mass UFO sightings ever recorded. Confirmed radar tracking, active military engagement, and thousands of civilian witnesses. More than 1,400 anti-aircraft rounds were fired. No aircraft was ever identified or recovered, fueling decades of declassified investigation and renewed modern UAP interest.
  • The Roswell Incident (1947)The case that launched the modern UFO era and remains a cornerstone of disclosure debate.
Part Three · Modern Military Encounters

Radar data, trained pilots, and official documents.

The modern record is built on evidence that did not exist in earlier centuries. Radar tracking, infrared sensor footage, trained military observers, and formal incident reports.

Cases the Pentagon has officially confirmed.

  • Rendlesham Forest (1980)Often called Britain's Roswell. Documented by multiple U.S. and British military personnel stationed at RAF Woodbridge.
  • The Phoenix Lights (1997)A mass sighting over Phoenix, Arizona witnessed by thousands of residents and acknowledged by Arizona state officials.
  • USS Nimitz Tic Tac Encounter (2004)Perhaps the most-documented modern UAP case, involving Navy pilot Commander David Fravor and others, captured on infrared targeting footage.
  • USS Roosevelt UAP IncidentsRepeated pilot encounters off the East Coast of the United States, accompanied by formal pilot safety reports.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything readers ask.

What is Ancient Aliens to Modern UFOs: The Disclosure Revolution about?
It is a meticulously researched non-fiction investigation by Adil Bhatti connecting ancient astronaut evidence, biblical and religious UFO encounters, declassified military files, Pentagon-confirmed UAP footage, and historic Congressional testimony into one continuous narrative. The book walks readers from Sumerian Anunnaki accounts, Egyptian star-aligned monuments, Amarna-period sculptures, Ezekiel's wheels, Hindu scripture, the Quran, and the Nazca Lines, forward through the 1561 Nuremberg celestial event, the 1896 Great Airship Mystery, the 1942 Battle of Los Angeles, Roswell, Rendlesham Forest, the Phoenix Lights, the USS Nimitz Tic Tac incident, USS Roosevelt UAP incidents, AATIP, To The Stars Academy, Pentagon-confirmed Navy UAP footage, and the 2023 Congressional hearings with Ryan Graves, David Grusch, and Commander David Fravor.
Is this book fiction or non-fiction?
Non-fiction. The book is built on declassified government documents, historical sightings, Congressional UAP testimony under oath, Pentagon-confirmed footage, and verified eyewitness accounts. Written for readers who want research-based material rather than speculative fiction.
Does the book discuss the implications of disclosure for energy, economics, and society?
Yes. The book examines the broader stakes of genuine disclosure. If the propulsion technology behind UFO and UAP sightings is real, it likely operates on energy principles outside the fossil-fuel and petroleum-driven economy, raising serious questions about suppressed technology, capitalist incentives, and the future of global energy. The book also considers how confirmation of non-human intelligence would challenge foundational religious assumptions and reshape humanity's understanding of its own origins.
What ancient civilizations and texts does the book cover?
The book covers Sumerian tablets describing the Anunnaki, Egyptian monuments aligned with the stars, Amarna-period sculptures from the reign of Akhenaten, Ezekiel's wheels within wheels, Hindu scripture and its accounts of celestial beings and advanced vehicles, references to celestial beings in the Quran, and the Nazca Lines of Peru which were designed to be seen from the air.
Does the book cover the Nazca Lines?
Yes. The Nazca Lines of Peru are covered as part of the ancient evidence section. The book examines why a pre-modern civilization constructed geoglyphs visible only from the air, and what that may imply about what they were depicting or communicating with.
Does the book discuss Akhenaten and the Amarna period?
Yes. During Akhenaten's reign approximately 3,300 years ago, Egyptian royal art shifted almost overnight to depict elongated skulls, narrow faces, and proportions unseen before or after. The book presents this Amarna-period anomaly alongside ongoing discussions of symbolism, genetics, and theories that these depictions may reflect encounters preserved in stone.
Does the book cover the 1942 Battle of Los Angeles?
Yes. The 1942 Battle of Los Angeles is covered as one of the most significant mass UFO sightings ever recorded. The event involved confirmed radar tracking, active military engagement, and thousands of civilian witnesses. The U.S. military fired more than 1,400 anti-aircraft rounds and failed to identify or recover any aircraft, fueling decades of declassified investigation and renewed modern UAP interest.
Does the book cover the 1896-1897 Great Airship Mystery?
Yes. The Great Airship Mystery of 1896 to 1897 is covered, including the waves of mysterious airship sightings across the United States at a time when no such aircraft existed.
Does it cover the USS Nimitz Tic Tac UFO incident?
Yes. The 2004 USS Nimitz Tic Tac incident, involving Navy pilot Commander David Fravor and other witnesses, is covered in detail as part of the modern disclosure arc.
Does the book discuss the USS Roosevelt UAP incidents?
Yes. The USS Roosevelt UAP incidents and the pilot safety reports that followed are examined as part of the modern military record.
Who is Luis Elizondo and what is AATIP?
Luis Elizondo is the former intelligence official credited with publicly exposing the existence of AATIP, the Advanced Aerospace Threat Identification Program, a classified Pentagon effort to investigate UAP. Elizondo's role in bringing AATIP to public attention is a central part of the modern disclosure timeline.
Who is Tom DeLonge and what is To The Stars Academy?
Tom DeLonge is the founder of To The Stars Academy (TTSA), an organization that played a pivotal role in the public release and mainstream legitimization of Pentagon UAP footage. The book covers TTSA's contribution to the modern disclosure arc.
What happened at the 2023 Congressional UAP hearings?
The 2023 Congressional UAP hearings featured sworn testimony from Ryan Graves, David Grusch, and Commander David Fravor about UAP encounters, classified investigations, and unexplained technology operating in restricted airspace.
