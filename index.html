About the Author

Adil Bhatti.

Adil Bhatti is an American-Pakistani author whose work crosses continents, cultures, and dimensions. With his home in the United States, he has lived, traveled, and eaten his way through Türkiye, Morocco, Dubai, Japan, England, and far beyond, and that restless curiosity runs through everything he writes. He is a lifelong lover of science fiction, space, and the ancient questions that have followed humanity from the first time we looked up.

His catalog ranges from young adult sci-fi adventure, to investigative non-fiction on UFOs and ancient mysteries, to boldly designed notebooks for readers who want their stationery to say something. At the center of all of it: family, and food worth traveling for.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything readers ask, answered.

Who is Adil Bhatti?
Adil Bhatti is an American-Pakistani author based in the United States. He writes young adult science fiction, investigative non-fiction on UFOs and ancient mysteries, and publishes bold Islamic notebooks for Muslim readers. His debut novel, Omar & Ali: The Key to Orulenthia, was released June 28, 2025. He publishes under his independent imprint, Alpha Leonis Production Studio LLC.
What books has Adil Bhatti written?
Adil Bhatti has written two full-length books: Omar & Ali: The Key to Orulenthia, a clean young adult sci-fi fantasy adventure with Muslim heroes released in June 2025, and Ancient Aliens to Modern UFOs: The Disclosure Revolution, an investigative non-fiction book on ancient astronaut theory and Pentagon UAP disclosure released in December 2025. He has also published ten bold Islamic notebooks.
What is Omar & Ali: The Key to Orulenthia about?
Omar & Ali: The Key to Orulenthia is a 243-page young adult sci-fi fantasy novel by Adil Bhatti, published June 28, 2025 under Alpha Leonis Production Studio LLC. It follows two Muslim American teenage brothers from New Jersey who activate an ancient triangular alien relic in Tangier, Morocco, and are pulled across space-time to the red-skied planet of Orulenthia. There, they join a rebellion led by the warrior-scholar Vaan against a tyrant ruling with an iron fist. Available in paperback, hardcover, and Kindle on Amazon, plus Barnes & Noble, Walmart, and independent bookstores through IngramSpark.
What is Ancient Aliens to Modern UFOs about?
It is an investigative non-fiction book that connects ancient astronaut theory, declassified Pentagon UAP files, biblical UFO encounters, and modern military testimony into one narrative. It covers cases including Roswell, the 1561 Nuremberg celestial event, the Battle of Los Angeles, Rendlesham Forest, the Phoenix Lights, and the USS Nimitz Tic Tac incident. Released December 2025.

Yes. Both Omar & Ali: The Key to Orulenthia and Ancient Aliens to Modern UFOs: The Disclosure Revolution are available as Kindle ebooks on Amazon. The notebooks are only available in physical paperback and hardcover formats, as they are designed for writing.
Are Adil Bhatti's books available in hardcover?
Yes. Both books and all ten notebooks are available in hardcover editions on Amazon, alongside paperback and Kindle where applicable.
What notebooks does Adil Bhatti sell?
Adil Bhatti publishes ten Islamic notebooks under his Alpha Leonis imprint. Five are designed for Muslim women and girls with hijabi-themed cover art (Hijabi 'til I Die, Hijabi Queen, Hijabi Gang, Hijabi and Chill, Hijabi and Unbreakable). Do Adil Bhatti's notebooks have Bismillah on every page?
Yes. Every one of the 200 college-ruled lined pages inside each notebook is adorned with the sacred phrase بِسْمِ ٱللَّٰهِ ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ (Bismillah ar-Rahman ar-Raheem) in elegant Arabic calligraphy at the top, a daily reminder to begin each thought, plan, or prayer with the name of Allah.
What size are the notebooks?
Each notebook measures 6.24 x 9.24 inches with a spine thickness of 0.64 inches. They contain 200 college-ruled lined pages and weigh 12.8 ounces. The size is comfortable for school bags, backpacks, and desk use.
Are these notebooks good for Ramadan or Eid gifts?
Yes. The notebooks are designed specifically with Ramadan, Eid, and Jummah gifting in mind. Their bold cover art and Bismillah-adorned pages make them meaningful presents for Islamic school students, Muslim college brothers and sisters, prayer journal enthusiasts, and anyone who wants their stationery to reflect their faith.
What genre are Adil Bhatti's books?
Omar & Ali: The Key to Orulenthia is young adult science fiction and portal fantasy, fitting readers of Percy Jackson, A Wrinkle in Time, and Star Wars. Ancient Aliens to Modern UFOs is investigative non-fiction covering UFO disclosure, ancient astronaut theory, religious and biblical UFO encounters, and modern military UAP reports.
Is Omar & Ali suitable for teens and young adults?
Yes. Omar & Ali: The Key to Orulenthia is written specifically for teens and young adults and classified as clean Halal Entertainment. It contains no romance, profanity, or graphic violence, and is crafted to be appropriate for Muslim readers while appealing to fantasy fans of all backgrounds.

Yes. It is written for both newcomers and serious researchers and is not speculation or fiction.
